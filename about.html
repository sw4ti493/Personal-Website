Writing, Communication, User Experience

Before getting started in web development, I spent some time as a digital marketing consultant for several different startups around the country and was able to take the time to understand the importance of easy-to-navigate design, but also the necessity of great writing and communication style for a truly fantastic user experience.

Along with what I have (and will continue to) learn about development, these prior experiences are a great asset in terms of being intuitive about the best way to create website content for maximum conversion.
